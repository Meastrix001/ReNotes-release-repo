# ReNotes

A free e sticky notes app for Windows. Each note lives in its own small always-on-top window, and notes sync across your devices with end-to-end encryption.

## Features

- Individual floating windows per note, pinnable and color-coded
- Optional account with encrypted cloud sync across devices
- Per-note password lock for sensitive notes
- Full-text search across your notes
- Works fully offline, no account required
- Auto-updates
- Available for Windows

## Why ReNotes

Most sticky notes apps either keep everything local (so you lose your notes when you switch machines) or store your content in plain text on someone else's server. ReNotes encrypts every note on your device before it ever leaves, using a key derived from your password. Not even the server can read them.
